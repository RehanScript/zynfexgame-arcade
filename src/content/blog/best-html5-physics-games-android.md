## 🧠 What Makes a Good HTML5 Physics Game?

Not all games are created equal—especially on low-end devices.

Here’s what actually matters:

### ✅ Lightweight Design
Heavy graphics = lag  
Simple visuals = smooth gameplay  

### ✅ Fast Loading
If it takes forever to load, it’s not worth it  

### ✅ Responsive Controls
Physics games depend on timing—lag ruins everything  

### ✅ Mobile Optimization
Touch controls should feel natural, not clunky  

---

## ⚙️ Tips to Play Smoothly on Low-End Android

Even lightweight games can lag if your phone isn’t optimized.

Here’s what actually helps (and yes, these make a difference):

- Close background apps before playing  
- Use Chrome or a lightweight browser like Brave  
- Clear cache regularly  
- Avoid playing on very slow networks  
- Turn off battery saver mode while gaming  

Small tweaks—but they add up.
