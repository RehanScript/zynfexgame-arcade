👉 Ideal for gamers who love exploring new games.

## 🧠 What is Poki?

Poki is a curated online gaming platform focused on delivering high-quality, user-friendly HTML5 games.

- Hand-picked games  
- Mobile-optimized experience  
- Minimal ads  

👉 Perfect for casual and mobile gamers.
