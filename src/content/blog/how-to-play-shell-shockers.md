## 🧠 How to Aim Better (Simple Tricks That Work)

This is where most players struggle.

### 🎯 1. Aim for the Yolk (Headshots)
Headshots deal more damage.

👉 Always aim slightly above the body.

---

### 🎯 2. Don’t Panic Shoot
Take a split second to aim properly.

---

### 🎯 3. Use Short Bursts
Instead of holding fire:
- Shoot in short bursts  
- Keep your aim steady  

---

### 🎯 4. Adjust Sensitivity
If your aim feels shaky:
- Lower sensitivity slightly  

👉 This alone can improve accuracy instantly.

---

## ⚡ Movement Tips (Stay Alive Longer)

Good movement = fewer deaths.

### 🏃 Keep Moving Always
Never stand still—not even while shooting.

---

### 🧱 Use Cover Smartly
Peek, shoot, hide.

---

### 🌀 Jump Occasionally
Jumping makes you harder to hit.

---

### 🔄 Strafe (Side Movement)
Move left and right while shooting.

👉 This is HUGE in winning gunfights.

Lag kills performance—especially on low-end devices.

### ⚡ Fix Lag Easily:

- Close background apps  
- Use Chrome or Brave browser  
- Lower graphics settings  
- Use stable internet  
- Avoid too many tabs
